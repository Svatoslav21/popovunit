Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Tommy Bailey ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by AutoWallet.

1. Information Collection

AutoWallet is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect personal data that identifies you as an individual unless you voluntarily provide it to us (e.g., via support emails).

  • Usage Data: All expense data, mileage logs, and notes entered into the app are stored locally on your device.
  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account to use the core features of the application.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since the data you input is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to your financial records or driving history. The app uses this data solely to:

  • Display your expense history
  • Generate statistical charts and analytics
  • Calculate averages and totals

3. Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you (e.g., for crash reporting or analytics to improve app stability). Please refer to the privacy policies of any third-party service providers used by the app.

4. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
